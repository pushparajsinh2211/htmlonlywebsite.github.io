THE THOR

''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''



The son of Odin uses his mighty abilities as the God of Thunder to protect his home Asgard and planet Earth alike.
Thor Odinson wields the power of ancient Asgardians to fight evil throughout the Nine Realms and beyond.

_______________________________________________

POWER AND ABILITIES

''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''

  • Mjolnir : Thor’s signature weapon is Mjolnir, which he uses to summon rain, wind, lightning, and thunder, and also allows him to blast or contain energy beams. The hammer can only be lifted y those worthy of its power.
  • Superhuman Strength : As a God, Thor ages at an incredibly slow pace, allowing him to live for millennia. Similarly, he possesses the stamina, durability, and speed of a divine warrior, and is among the strongest beings in the universe.
  • Hand-to-Hand : Thor Odinson is a gifted combatant. His ancient skills and experience in combat pair with his enormous might to make him one of the finest fighters in existence.

1. THOR

        As the son of Odin, king of the Norse gods, Thor will soon inherit the throne of Asgard from his aging father. However, on the day that he is
        to be crowned, Thor reacts with brutality when the gods' enemies, the Frost Giants, enter the palace in violation of their treaty. As
        punishment, Odin banishes Thor to Earth. While Loki, Thor's brother, plots mischief in Asgard, Thor, now stripped of his powers, 
        faces his greatest threat.

_______________________________________________


2. THOR : THE DARK WORLD

        In the aftermath of Marvel's "Thor" and  Marvel's "The Avengers", Thor fights to restore order across the cosmos...but an ancient race led by
        the vengeful Malekith returns to plunge the universe back into darkness. Faced with an enemy that even Odin and Asgard cannot withstand, 
        Thor must embark on his most perilous and personal journey yet, one that will reunite him with Jane Foster and force him to sacrifice 
        everything to save us all.

_______________________________________


3. THOR : RAGNAROK

        Thor is imprisoned on the other side of the universe without his mighty hammer and finds himself in a race against time to get back to Asgard 
        to stop Ragnarok—the destruction of his homeworld and the end of Asgardian civilization—at the hands of an all-powerful new threat, 
        the ruthless Hela. But first he must survive a deadly gladiatorial contest that pits him against his former ally and fellow 
        Avenger—the Incredible Hulk!
